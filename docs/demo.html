Scrolling Zone

Scroll gesture test window: Raise index, middle, ring, and pinky fingers up to scroll this text box.

Move your hand up above the vertical center to scroll up, or down below to scroll down.

Gesconi measures the palm coordinate (middle MCP landmark) relative to the screen deadzone to determine the direction and speed of scroll steps.

OpenCV and MediaPipe enable fluid hands-free reading without physical mouse wheel rotation.

This sandbox simulates the exact system scroll commands sent via PyAutoGUI scroll functions in desktop mode.

You can customize the scroll speed and deadzone boundaries directly inside `gesconi.py` configurations.

Test scrolling all the way to the bottom to verify it behaves correctly!

Key Typing (Fist triggers)
Close your fist (pauses tracking), then open index to type 'a', index+middle for 'b', or index+middle+ring for 'c'.
